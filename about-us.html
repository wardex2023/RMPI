About Us

Integrating Engineering with Quality and Innovation

RPMI is one of the leading contractors in Leyte providing unparalleled Mechanical, Civil, Structural, Electrical and Instrumentation services across various industries.

Our roots goes back to 2013, where Romelo Mappala founded the company at Simangan, Isabel, Leyte initially venturing into General Sales or Trading (Wholesale or Retail) of light motor vehicle and heavy equipment parts, accessories and supplies. Later on, it’s main office was moved to Libertad, Isabel, Leyte which is still utilized ‘til present. The company has grown over the years and is now operating in other parts of the country with a track record of success continuously striving to deliver safe, well-executed,capital-efficient and high-quality standard projects to our clients.

Over the 10 years of operation, we have successfully delivered and completed a lot of projects for renowned companies in the Philippines such as Seaoil Philippines Inc., PASAR Corporation, Aboitiz Power Corporation, Palm Concepcion Power Corporation, Aggreko Energy Rental Solutions Inc., Mechatrends Contractors Corporation (MCC), Lide Management Corporation (LMC) and a lot more.

Behind all of this success is a team of highly-skilled professional engineers, skilled workers and highly-dedicated management staff with diverse backgrounds and extensive experience that thrives to deliver outstanding results. Our collective expertise, combined with a shared commitment to excellence, drives the success of our projects.

RP MAPPALA INCORPORATED will continue its role as a responsible Filipino Contractor executing quality and reliable Construction & Industrial Services while continually rewarding its clients and employees.

SAFETY AND ENVIRONMENTAL STEWARDSHIP

RPMI ensures that safety and quality are paramount to our operations. We adhere to the highest industry standards and regulations by conducting appropriate and continuous trainings to ensure safety and health for our workers, and that all our projects are executed with the utmost precision and security.

Certifications and Corporate Memberships

It Enables the Management to Track/Monitor all of its assets.
